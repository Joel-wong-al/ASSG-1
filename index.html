EVE

Eve is a Japanese singer-songwriter, producer, and former Vocaloid producer, renowned for his unique style blending pop, rock, and experimental elements. He began his career on the video-sharing platform Niconico, covering Vocaloid songs, before transitioning to creating original music. Known for his mysterious persona and visually stunning music videos, Eve rarely shows his face publicly, letting his art and animations take center stage.
